Long ago near Loch Corraillí, a terrible creature known as An Phiast terrorised the land, devouring livestock and spreading fear among the people. One Day, St. Cuan faced the monster and trapped it beneath the lake by placing a great black pot upon its head, forcing it back into the dark caves below.

Years have passed, but the caves remain dangerous and mysterious. Strange creatures roam the tunnels, and an Phiast must face the challenges of the underground world to find a way out.
